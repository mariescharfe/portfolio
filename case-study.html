Simple Split

In a group of 3, in which I was the project lead, I guided the team as we came over challenges and found solutions while developping brand identity and logo for Simple Split. Simple Split is an app that helps parents, cohabitors, and partners living together manage their finances, track and share expenses, store receipts and print annual report for taxes.

V

Process

Observations

The design process involved determining an appropriate identity for Simple Split, which brought our client's vision to life. We started by determining the target audience, and from there started the sketching process.

We created a logo, brand guidelines and a SASS website prototype for the app. The fonts and colours chosen helped create a soft and simple identity.

With proper and extensive research, we were able to create a unified brand for Simple Split. Throughout this process I developped leadership and communication skills.
